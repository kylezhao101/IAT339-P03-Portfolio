Project Overview

In this project, I utilized Youtube-DL, Asyncio, and Discord.py to create a multi-threaded, Youtube and Soundcloud music music player bot for users to listen and manipulate through their Discord applications using text commands.

With MongoDB integration, bot users are able manage and play their favorite songs.

As this is one of my first coding projects, I learned how to Google effectively in order to learn and debug new and unfamilliar APIs. Throughout the process of creating this bot, I further gained knowledge in these APIs through reading documentation, viewing examples and looking for solutions to my issues on stackoverflow.

Issues

This bot uses Youtube-dl to scrape and retrieve audio files from Youtube.com, however it has been abandoned as of 2021.12.17. The current bot uses a fork of Youtube-dl, yt-dlp which continues to adapt as the official Youtube site changes. Thus, my bot requires constant use and maintainance, which involves bug-fixing and updating code whenever Youtube makes changes.
